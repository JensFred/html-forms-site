Frequently Asked Questions

Income protection is a type of insurance that pays out a regular income if you're unable to work due to illness or injury.

Costs vary based on your job, health, income, and policy terms—but you can get covered from as little as £10/month.

It typically starts after your chosen deferred period (e.g., 4, 8, or 13 weeks) and continues until you recover or the policy ends.
